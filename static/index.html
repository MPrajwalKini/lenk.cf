An Open-Source free to use URL shortner.

Domains assigined:

https://lenk.cf

https://nani.cf

https://4543.ml

https://urml.ml (slower)

Note: You can use the slug of the shorterned URL's with any of above domains.

i.e. If your URL is 4543.ml/hi it will even work with lenk.cf/hi
